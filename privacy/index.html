Privacy Policy

Zeraya Privacy Policy

Effective date: 6 July 2026

Zeraya is made by Inspirante Pte. Ltd. (“we,” “us”). This policy is short because our approach is simple: your data stays on your device. We do not collect anything.

1. What we collect: nothing.

We do not collect, receive, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app has no accounts, no login, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs that receive your data. The app makes no network connections of its own; the only network activity is Apple’s own App Store and purchase system.

2. What stays on your device.

To generate your readings, the app asks for your name, birth date, and — optionally — birth time, birth place, and an intention, and lets you save people for relationship reflections. All of this is stored only in the app’s local storage on your device. It is used solely to generate readings on your device. It never leaves your device, and we have no way to access it.

3. Purchases.

Subscriptions and purchases are processed entirely by Apple through your Apple account. We never see your payment details. Apple’s handling of purchase data is described in Apple’s privacy policy. Your purchase entitlement is read from Apple’s system on your device; we operate no server that records it.

4. Deleting your data.

Because everything lives on your device, deleting the app deletes all of its data. You can also erase everything without deleting the app using Settings → Reset All Data inside the app. There is nothing for us to delete on any server, because no server exists.

5. Children.

Zeraya is designed for adults reflecting on their own lives and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone — including children — because we collect no data at all.

6. Your rights.

Privacy laws in various regions (such as GDPR and CCPA) grant rights over personal data that companies hold about you. We hold none. If you have questions about this policy, contact us and we will answer.

7. Changes to this policy.

If a future version of the app ever changes this approach (for example, optional cloud sync), we will update this policy first, state clearly what changed, and any new data feature will be opt-in.

8. Contact.

Inspirante Pte. Ltd. — support@zeraya.app

9. Governing law.

This policy is governed by the laws of Singapore.

星盘知己（Zeraya）由 Inspirante Pte. Ltd.（下称“我们”）开发。本政策之所以简短，是因为我们的原则很简单：你的数据只属于你的设备。我们不收集任何信息。

1. 我们收集什么：什么都不收集。

我们不收集、不接收、不传输、不出售、也不共享任何个人数据。应用没有账户、没有登录、没有分析统计、没有追踪、没有广告，也没有任何会接收你数据的第三方组件。应用自身不进行任何网络连接；唯一的网络活动来自 Apple 的 App Store 与购买系统。

为了生成你的解读，应用会请你填写姓名、出生日期，以及可选的出生时间、出生地点和一个意向，并允许你保存用于关系映照的人物信息。所有这些内容仅保存在你设备上的应用本地存储中，仅用于在你的设备上生成解读。它们不会离开你的设备，我们也没有任何途径访问它们。

订阅与购买完全由 Apple 通过你的 Apple 账户处理。我们不会看到你的付款信息。Apple 对购买数据的处理方式见 Apple 的隐私政策。你的订阅权益由你设备上的 Apple 系统读取；我们没有任何服务器记录它。

4. 删除你的数据。

因为一切都保存在你的设备上，删除应用即删除其全部数据。你也可以在应用内通过 设置 → 重置全部数据 清除所有内容而无需删除应用。我们的服务器上没有任何可删除的内容——因为服务器并不存在。

5. 儿童。

星盘知己面向反思自身生活的成年人，并非为儿童设计。我们不会在知情的情况下收集任何人的数据——包括儿童——因为我们根本不收集数据。

6. 你的权利。

各地区的隐私法律（如 GDPR、CCPA）赋予你对企业所持个人数据的权利。我们不持有任何此类数据。如你对本政策有疑问，欢迎联系我们。

7. 政策变更。

如果未来版本改变这一做法（例如可选的云端同步），我们会先更新本政策，清楚说明变化内容，并且任何新的数据功能都将由你主动选择开启。
